What it does

Everything you need, nothing you don't

A guided six-step builder that handles all the maths — so you can focus on the story.

📜

Guided 6-Step Builder

Walk through race, class, background, ability scores, and equipment with a clean, opinionated UI.

⚔️

Full Combat Sheet

AC, initiative, hit points, attacks, spell slots, saving throws, and skill modifiers — all computed automatically.

🖨️

PDF Export

Print your finished sheet directly from the browser. No third-party tools, no uploads.

🌍

Ryndor-Exclusive Content

Custom races, classes, subclasses, and backgrounds drawn straight from the Weirded Lands sourcebook.

🎲

AI Random Roll

One click generates a complete character — stats, equipment, skills — and uses Claude AI to write a name and personality.

💾

Save & Resume

Export your character as JSON and pick up exactly where you left off in any future session.

Powered by Claude

🎲 Roll Random Character

Can't decide? One button generates a complete, playable character in seconds.

1

Randomise Everything

Race, class, subclass, background, alignment, and a shuffled Standard Array across all six stats.

2

Pick Skills & Gear

Class skill options and equipment choices are sampled automatically, avoiding conflicts with racial and background grants.

3

Claude Writes the Story

The Anthropic API generates a fitting name, personality, ideals, bonds, and flaws based on the rolled combination.

4

Jump to the Sheet

The fully computed character sheet is ready instantly — print it, save it, or keep rolling.
